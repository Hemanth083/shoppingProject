About Blue Salamander

Our mission is to study and experience cultures from around the world through comfort food. Blue Salamander is one way for us to document our travels through healthy flavors from across the globe. Unlike a typical online store we launched our website prior to offering our products for sale. Our International low calorie snack Blog has documented our exploration of treats that we now offer and some we will offer in the future. Our international snack store was launched at the beginning of 2022. We currently feature nearly 1,000 snacks from 12+ countries and offer their best diet snacks.
